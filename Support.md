## Common questions

### How do I cancel my subscription or free trial?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple, not by us. To cancel:

1. Open the **Settings** app on your iPhone or iPad.
2. Tap your **name** at the top.
3. Tap **Subscriptions**.
4. Tap **WiFi Buddy Pro** and choose **Cancel Subscription**.

If you cancel during the free trial period, you will not be charged.

### How do I restore a previous purchase?

Open the app, go to the **Pro** tab, and tap **Restore Purchase**. Make
sure you're signed in to the App Store with the Apple ID you used to
buy the subscription.

### Do you collect my data?

No. WiFi Buddy does not run servers, accounts, analytics, or ads. All
your survey history, device list, trust flags, and Klaus chat history
live **only on your device**. See the
[Privacy Policy](https://wifibuddy.app/privacy) for details.

### Why does the Survey tab need camera access?

The Survey tab uses **ARKit world tracking** to convert your real
walking path into a position on the floor plan. The camera feed is
only used by ARKit for visual-inertial tracking — it is **never
recorded, screenshotted, or transmitted**.

### Why does the Devices tab need Local Network access?

The Devices tab discovers devices on your home Wi-Fi using Bonjour,
SSDP, and TCP port probes. iOS requires the **Local Network**
permission for any of those to work. None of that traffic leaves your
router.
